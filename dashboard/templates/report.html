About this Pipeline

TalentDash is an AI-powered salary data engineering pipeline that continuously collects salary information from public sources, normalizes the data using Gemini LLM, validates every field with Pydantic, standardizes company names and job levels, stores verified records in Neon PostgreSQL, and removes duplicate salary submissions before making them available through the dashboard.
